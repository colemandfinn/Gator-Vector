Block Counting

AFOQT spatial drill — count how many blocks the marked block touches. Unofficial study aid.

A contact is a shared face — above, below, or any side. A diagonal or edge-only touch does not count.

Timed Drill: 30 items, 4:30 clock, no feedback — real test conditions.
Practice: untimed, immediate feedback showing the contact breakdown; go as long as you like.
